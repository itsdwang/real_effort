Instructions

In this study, you will be in a randomly formed group of {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants. Each participant in the group has a fixed income of {{ Constants.endowment }}. The group has the opportunity to undertake a joint project. A tax of {{ Constants.tax }} is automatically collected from each player. Each participant in the group decides how much of his/her income to report. Reported income could be any integer from 0 to {{ Constants.endowment }}.

The earnings from the project are calculated as follows: The total tax collected from all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants are added up, the total tax is multiplied by a factor of {{ Constants.multiplier }}, and the resulting amount is the total earnings from the project, which is evenly split among all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants. Your payoff equals your earnings from the project, plus the amount of income you have remaining after the tax is taken out.
